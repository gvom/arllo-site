Welcome to the world of artificial intelligence with Apollo, your innovative and highly versatile virtual assistant! If you’re looking for an AI solution that meets all your needs, you’ve come to the right place. Let us show you why Apollo is the perfect choice to enhance your digital experiences!

Online mode

Utilizes artificial intelligence models that are hosted and run on remote servers in the cloud, reducing installation requirements and running faster, using state-of-the-art AI models.

Offline mode

Run exclusively on the user’s local device, completely independent of internet connectivity. This mode offers significant advantages, including freedom from reliance on internet access, bolstered privacy and security, and liberation from any cloud-based limitations.

Speech mode

Whether it’s through text or voice, Apollo is always ready to answer your questions and perform tasks quickly and accurately

Agent mode

A agent that makes intelligent decisions based on the AI model you’ve chosen. Apollo can carry out tasks autonomously, making your daily life more productive and efficient.

A virtual assistant wherever and whenever you need it

With Apollo, you have the freedom to choose between online and offline modes. In online mode, you can leverage the most advanced and widely used AI models available in the cloud. On the other hand, in offline mode, you can select from various AI models and opt for the one that best suits your needs and preferences.
